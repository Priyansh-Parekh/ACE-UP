Common Payment & Billing Issues

1. Network Problem During Payment

If you experience a network issue while making a payment, try the following:

  • Check your internet connection and try again.
  • Ensure your bank server is not down.
  • Use an alternative payment method.

2. Billing Process Confusion

Follow these steps to understand how ACE UP processes your payment:

  • Check your email for the payment confirmation receipt.
  • Log into your ACE UP account and go to "Billing History."
  • Contact your bank if the transaction is pending for too long.

3. Payment Refund Issues

If you haven't received your refund, check the following:

  • Ensure your refund request was submitted correctly.
  • Refunds may take 3-7 business days to process.
  • If the issue persists, contact ACE UP support.
